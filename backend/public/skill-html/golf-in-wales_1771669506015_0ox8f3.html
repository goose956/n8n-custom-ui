The Beauty of Golf in Wales

Golf in Wales boasts a rich history, a diverse range of courses, and stunning landscapes. With over 200 golf courses scattered throughout the country, including spectacular links courses by the sea, Wales is a golfer's paradise.

Why Choose Wales for Golfing?

Wales offers a unique golfing experience that combines challenging courses with jaw-dropping scenery. Courses like Newport Golf Club and Langland Bay Golf Club showcase the best of Welsh architecture, designed to challenge players while providing a beautiful backdrop.

Top Golf Courses to Explore

  • Royal Porthcawl: This championship course is one of the finest in the UK.
  • Southerndown: Known for its breathtaking views and challenging layout.
  • Pyle & Kenfig: A true links experience that impresses all who visit.
  • Celtic Manor: Famous for hosting the 2010 Ryder Cup.
  • Aberdovey: A hidden gem that offers a unique challenge.

Golf Tourism in Wales

With its compact nature, Wales allows for diverse golfing experiences in one trip. Whether you prefer coastal links or inland parkland courses, each day can bring a new adventure. The country is accessible year-round, thanks to its well-drained courses that remain playable even in wet conditions. Combining stunning landscapes with quality accommodations, Wales has become a must-visit destination for golfers from around the world.
